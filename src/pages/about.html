About Cavalry Manufacturing

Cavalry Manufacturing is a leader in advanced injection molding, producing high-quality firearm accessories, components, and Cavalry Medical products. Our solutions are trusted by shooting sports enthusiasts, military personnel, and law enforcement professionals nationwide.

With a focus on precision and durability, we engineer every product to meet strict performance standards. Whether you’re a professional agency, retailer, or individual enthusiast, Cavalry Manufacturing delivers reliability you can depend on.

Our mission is clear: to provide innovative, dependable products that empower our customers with confidence in every use. We continue to advance manufacturing techniques to keep pace with the needs of modern defense and sport.
