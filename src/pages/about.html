About Us

About Alaskans Art-n-Around

Alaskans Art-n-Around is a family-run traveling business from Juneau, Alaska. We journey across the U.S. in our RV, setting up at fairs and festivals to share a wide selection of artisan crafts — from mesmerizing laser-cut stainless steel wind spinners to healing stone jewelry, fossils, driftwood art, and handmade Navajo crafts.

Our mission is to bring peace, tranquility, and harmony into people’s lives through unique products inspired by nature, the earth, and ancient traditions. We pride ourselves on offering one of the largest selections of high-quality wind spinners in the U.S., complete with spinning tails that captivate and calm.

“We believe in connecting people with the earth’s beauty, history, and healing energy through carefully crafted art and jewelry.”
