About Us

About Midwest Flooring Suppliers

Midwest Flooring Suppliers is a family-owned business built on integrity, craftsmanship, and decades of hands-on experience in the flooring industry. We proudly supply durable, stylish flooring solutions for homes and commercial spaces across the Midwest.

What sets us apart? We source products directly from the manufacturer—saving our customers up to 40% compared to big-box retailers. Our team also handles every installation directly, never relying on subcontractors, to ensure consistent quality and accountability from start to finish.

I’m Patrick Bisimwa, founder of Midwest Flooring Suppliers. From day one, my goal has been to provide honest pricing, dependable service, and a better customer experience than the big chains. We treat every project like it’s our own—and that’s a promise we stand behind every day.

Patrick Bisimwa Founder & CEO
