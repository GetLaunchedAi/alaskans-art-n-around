About Us

About Alaskans Art-n-Around

Based in Juneau, Alaska, we are a traveling business bringing handcrafted art, jewelry, and healing items to fairs and festivals across the U.S.

Our mission is to share peace, tranquility, and harmony through our products — whether it's a dazzling wind spinner, a piece of ancient fossil, or jewelry wrapped with love and care.

We believe in quality, connection, and creating an unforgettable shopping experience wherever our travels take us.
